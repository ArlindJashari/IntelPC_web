EARNING DRAWING ENTRIES

Drawing entries may be earned during the Promotion from successful completion of the following activities:

(1) Weekly Trainings

New training modules will load on the Intel® Retail Edge Program Partner Showcase each week of the Promotion Period (each a “Weekly Training” or “Training”. The training schedule is as follows:

  • Week 1 (beginning June 3, 2026): four (4) Trainings for completion
  • Week 2 (beginning June 10, 2026): four (4) Trainings for completion
  • Week 3 (beginning June 17, 2026): three (3) Trainings for completion
  • Week 4 (beginning June 24, 2026): three (3) Trainings for completion
  • Week 5 (beginning July 1, 2026): two (2) Trainings for completion
  • Week 6 (beginning July 8, 2026): two (2) Trainings for completion

Drawing entry value: Each successfully completed Weekly Training earns the Participant one (1) entry into that week’s Weekly Drawing. Weekly Drawing entries apply only to the Weekly Drawing held for the specific week in which the Training was completed and do not carry over to future Weekly Drawings.

Maximum Weekly Drawing entries per Participant per week: four (4) in Weeks 1 and 2; three (3) in Weeks 3 and 4; and two (2) in Weeks 5 and 6.

Maximum total weekly trainings available throughout the Promotion: eighteen (18).

Grand Prize Qualification Requirement: To be eligible for entry into the Individual Grand Prize Drawing, a Participant must complete ALL eighteen (18) Weekly Trainings during the Promotion Period. Completion of all Weekly Trainings earns the Participant one (1) base entry into the Individual Grand Prize Drawing.


(2) Individual Challenges

Seven (7) Individual Challenges (each an “Individual Challenge” and collectively “Individual Challenges”) will be available during the Promotion Period.   • b. Norton Challenge — Complete the two (2) designated Norton-branded Trainings during the Promotion Period. Auto-validated upon completion of both trainings.
  • c. Acer Challenge — Complete the one (1) designated Acer-branded Training during the Promotion Period. Auto-validated upon completion of the Training.
  • d. ASUS Challenge — Complete the one (1) designated ASUS-branded training during the Promotion Period. Auto-validated upon completion of the Training.
  • e. Team Photo Challenge — Upload a photo of your in-store team that includes at least 3 total teammates. This can include retail teammates or OEM brand teammates. Submission requires manual review by Sponsor or its designee. Challenge is credited only when the submission is set to Approved status. Note: Photos will not be judged in any way.
